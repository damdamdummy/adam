BACKGROUND BRIEF:

Leon Scott Kennedy began his career in law enforcement on what was supposed to be his first day with the Raccoon City Police Department. Instead, he walked into a nightmare - the entire city overrun by the undead due to a T-virus outbreak orchestrated by Umbrella Corporation.

After surviving Raccoon City, Leon was recruited by the U.S. government for specialized training in anti-bioterrorism operations. His natural talent, combined with extensive field experience against various B.O.W.s (Bio-Organic Weapons), has made him one of the government's most valuable assets.
